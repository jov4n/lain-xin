Domains sit idle more often than they should. **lain.xin** was one of them.

This site is the correction: a sharp-edged surface for experiments, build notes, and the work that doesn’t fit a neat product page. Maya Unified, Infinite Fusion MMO, open source scraps, services — they all land here as signals, not press releases.

If you’re reading this, the lab is transmitting. Expect incomplete systems, honest status, and design that doesn’t apologize for hard edges.
