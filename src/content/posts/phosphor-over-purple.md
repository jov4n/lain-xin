Most “tech” sites borrow the same costume: soft cards, violet gradients, glow for glow’s sake. The lab refuses that costume.

**lain.xin** runs on graphite void, phosphor mint, and cold silver. Corners stay sharp. Type is Syne for presence and IBM Plex Mono for metadata. Motion is scarce and intentional — a brand that settles, a grid that drifts, sections that arrive.

Beauty here isn’t decoration. It’s the protocol.
