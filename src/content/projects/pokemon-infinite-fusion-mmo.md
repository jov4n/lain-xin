**Pokémon Infinite Fusion MMO** pushes fusion into a shared space: players, worlds, and systems that have to stay coherent when everyone is online at once.

The stack is still forming — netcode, creature state, world persistence — but the intent is clear: a high-tech playground where fusion isn’t a single-player gimmick, it’s a living network.

### Current focus
- Core loop prototypes and session architecture
- Creature / fusion data pipelines
- Lab updates as public build notes

Expect rough edges. That’s the point of the experiment.
