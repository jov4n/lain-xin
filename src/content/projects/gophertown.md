**Gophertown** is a Towns-style multiplayer clone: presence, shared spaces, and the glue that keeps a small world coherent when more than one person is online.

Separate from the Infinite Fusion MMO track — a lighter multiplayer sandbox for testing social/world systems.

### Current focus
- Core multiplayer loop and presence
- Shared-space experiments in TypeScript
